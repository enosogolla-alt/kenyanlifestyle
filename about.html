What is Kenyan Lifestyle?

More than a brand. A movement rooted in Kenyan identity, culture and pride.

Our Story

Kenyan Lifestyle was born in Nairobi — a city that never sleeps, never stops creating. We started with one jersey and a vision: to put Kenyan culture on the world map through fashion.

Mission

To create premium streetwear that celebrates Kenyan identity — designed locally, worn globally. Every piece we make tells a story of where we come from and where we are going.

Vision

To be Africa's most recognized streetwear brand — a name that stands for quality, culture and community wherever it is worn in the world.
